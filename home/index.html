Hi! My name is Junhyoung Chung.

I am currently pursuing a master's degree in Statistics at Seoul National University under the supervision of Professor Gunwoong Park. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Statistics and Economics from Seoul National University.

My academic goal is to design statistical learning algorithms that address practical challenges while maintaining statistical efficiency, with a few sub-objectives:

  • Developing robust models for noisy data.
  • Devising novel methodologies that learn complex data.
  • Ensuring computational scalability for large datasets.
  • Providing statistical guidance to assess and guarantee the reliability of machine-learned systems.

Recently, my research has centered on probabilistic graphical models, especially on developing consistent structure learning algorithms that account for measurement error. I have also carried out applied research introducing statistical algorithms tailored to real-world problem solving.
